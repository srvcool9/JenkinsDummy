Our History

In 1998, Anurag Shrivastava had an idea, and the blueprint of an IT start-up was drawn.
The vision was to create a data-driven platform to benefit aspiring young professionals.
The outcome was Netlink – a value-added technology and business solutions provider that propels innovation and transformation.
With a mission to help companies reach peak performance with the technology solutions and services they need.
Today, Netlink has more than ten offices worldwide. Leverages streamlined processes, advanced systems, superior technology, and proven expertise to deliver robust IT solutions. Connect with us on LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube, and FaceBook.
